Hey there !

My name is Nkosiphendule Jwara and this is a tribute page for Naledi Dlamini. I decided to create this site because I want to show her how much we value her in our class for her leadership, commitment and her caring heart. She is a true hero to us all and we want her to know how much we appreciate her even though we don't show her sometimes.

The reason I say she is a leader is that I would see her every morning having a headache just because of us. She commits her self to helping us with Maths, Physics, Afrikaans, you name it! She always placed us first and this is a chance for us to place her first.

From me, I just want to say that may God bless her with many years and may she have a wonderful and successful life. Thank You.
