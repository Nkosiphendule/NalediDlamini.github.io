Naledi Dlamini ~

"I am Naledi Dlamini. I live in Umlazi, P section, in Durban, in KwaZulu Natal Province(State), South Africa. I'm a huge feminist. I love reading and science and I'm as curious as they come. I sometimes pretend to be Zara Larson [I LOVE HER]. I live with both my parents and my two brothers. I'm the eldest and the princess of my father's little kingdom. I love helping people and enjoy it too. I was named after the purple flower [Yolanda] that blooms in Portugal. I make it my mission to make sure everyone around me is happy. I'm a lonner, I guess, at least I've been told. I am an RCL and an SGB member, I help poor kids by donating to opharnages. I've assisted in multiple charity events. I assist in every possible way I can."
